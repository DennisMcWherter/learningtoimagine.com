About

I am a software engineer. I used to work with hardware and embedded systems. I now work with machine learning and data analysis.

I enjoy reading in my free time. Topics such as math, history, and economics are amongst my favorite.

I usually consume my information from physical books and paid journalism. I find most internet resources have a very low signal-to-noise ratio.

I believe technologists play an important role in society. It is worth contemplating the economic and societal impacts of our work.

I write to bring clarity to my thinking.
